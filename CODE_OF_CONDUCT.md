# Code of Conduct

CapyMeet aims to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially across language and culture differences.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the specific change being reviewed.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear reproduction steps, screenshots, logs, or test cases when relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, repeated off-topic comments, or deliberately disruptive behavior.

## Reporting

For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For conduct issues, contact the maintainers privately when possible. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when needed to protect the project.
